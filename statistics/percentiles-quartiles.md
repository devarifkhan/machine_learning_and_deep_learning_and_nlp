## What is a Percentile?

A **percentile** tells you what percentage of data falls **below** a certain value.

**Example:**
- You scored in the 90th percentile
- Meaning: You scored better than 90% of people

---

## Common Percentiles

| Percentile | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| 25th (P25) | 25% below, 75% above |
| 50th (P50) | 50% below, 50% above (Median) |
| 75th (P75) | 75% below, 25% above |
| 90th (P90) | 90% below, 10% above |

---

## Three Quartiles

| Quartile | Also Called | Meaning |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| **Q1** | 25th percentile | 25% data below |
| **Q2** | 50th percentile (Median) | 50% data below |
| **Q3** | 75th percentile | 75% data below |

## IQR (Interquartile Range)

```
IQR = Q3 - Q1

IQR = 84 - 46 = 38

This is the spread of the middle 50% of data.
```
