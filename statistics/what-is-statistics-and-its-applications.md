## What is Statistics?

**Statistics** is the science of learning from data. It helps us collect, organize, analyze, and interpret numbers to make smart decisions.

Think of it like this: You have 1 million customer reviews. You can't read all of them. Statistics helps you understand the *whole story* by looking at patterns and averages.
